About Rachel

A life devoted
to the keys

Rachel S. Morrison holds degrees from the San Francisco Conservatory and the Royal College of Music in London. With over fourteen years of private teaching experience and students placed at Juilliard, NEC, and Curtis, she brings both conservatory rigour and genuine warmth to every lesson.

Whether you're five years old discovering your first melody, a teenager preparing for conservatory auditions, or an adult finally finding time to play — Rachel's studio is your home.

The Process

Simple, personal,
joyful

1

Free Discovery Call

A relaxed 20-minute conversation about your goals, experience, and what you'd love to play. No pressure — just music.

2

Your Trial Lesson

A full 45-minute session where we play, explore, and design your personal musical roadmap together.

3

A Tailored Program

Your curriculum is built entirely around you — your pace, your favourite pieces, and the kind of musician you want to become.

4

Grow & Perform

Regular lessons, monthly progress reflections, and invitations to studio recitals where you can share what you've made.
