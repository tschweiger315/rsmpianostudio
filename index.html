About Tanya

A life devoted
to the keys

Tanya Schweiger holds Bachelor of Music and Master of Music degrees in classical piano performance from the University of Redlands, with further study under Cordelia Hoeffer at the world-renowned Mozarteum in Salzburg, Austria. For over twenty-five years she has worked with each student to reach their own goals at the instrument, bringing a signature blend of genuine warmth and high-level rigor to every lesson.

An award-winning performer who has appeared internationally as a soloist, ensemble musician, and orchestral soloist, Tanya is an active member of the Music Teachers' Association of California. Whether your student is a young beginner discovering their first melody or an adult finally finding time to play — Tanya's studio is your home.
